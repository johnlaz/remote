LMC Remote — what shipped

The whole room, not just the PC.

Everything new in the LMC build on top of the Media Remote foundation.

📺

TCL Google TV over ADB

Full d-pad, power, volume, mute, HDMI source switching, and a Gemini button — all over ADB Wireless Debugging. Quick-launch for Netflix, Prime, Hulu, YouTube, plus 4 customizable slots by Android package name.

🔍

mDNS auto-discovery

Finds the TV's live IP and ADB port automatically — even after it changes on Wi-Fi reconnect. No more reading the IP off the TV settings screen and typing it in manually every time.

🔊

Sonos control

Volume slider and mute for any Sonos speaker on the network. A multi-speaker picker discovers every available speaker so you're always controlling the right room.

💡

Shelly Wall Dimmer

Brightness slider, quick preset levels (Off / 10% / 20% / 50%), and full scene integration — Movie Night can auto-dim or cut the light, Easy Listening sets a fixed level, all from a single tap.

🎬

Movie Night & Easy Listening

One-tap scene buttons at the top of the Room tab. Independent toggles for Sonos mute and TV audio per scene — pause just the speakers, just the TV, or both. No automation config required.

📱

Installable PWA + unified backup

Add to your phone's home screen as a real app with a proper icon — not a browser bookmark. One JSON export covers all Room config and PC shortcuts together, so nothing gets left behind.

Setup

Three steps. No wizard.

One exe, one batch file. No background installer, no dependencies, nothing phoning home.

1

Download & unzip

Drop the folder anywhere on the media PC. MediaRemote.exe and Setup.bat just need to stay together.

2

Run Setup.bat

One UAC prompt adds the firewall rule, registers it to start with Windows, and drops a shortcut on your Desktop with the app icon.

3

Open on your phone

The tray icon shows your PC's address on hover. Open it in any browser — or install it as a PWA for the real home-screen experience.
